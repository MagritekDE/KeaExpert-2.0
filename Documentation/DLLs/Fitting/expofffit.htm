expofffit

 

 

Perform a single exponential fit with offset to the supplied data.

 

Syntax

 

(fit, Ea, Ta, Off, errEa, errTa, errOff) = expfit(x, y, [[noise], [report])

 

fit ...... best fit to x,y data (FLOAT)

Ea ....... amplitude of exponential (FLOAT)

Ta ....... time constant of exponential (FLOAT)

Off ...... offset (FLOAT)

errEa .... error in amplitude of exponential (FLOAT)

errTa .... error in time constant of exponential (FLOAT)

errOff ... error in offset (FLOAT)

x ........ x axis of supplied data (VEC)

y ........ y axis of supplied data (VEC)

noise .... rms noise of supplied data (FLOAT)

report ... whether to list all results in the CLI. (STR yes/no)
