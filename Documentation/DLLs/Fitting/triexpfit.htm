triexpfit

 

 

Perform a tri-exponential fit to the supplied data.

 

Syntax

 

(fit, Ea, Ta, Eb, Tb, Ec, Tc, errEa, errTa, errEb, errTb, errEc, errTc) = triexpfit(x, y, [[noise], [report])

 

fit ...... best fit to x,y data (FLOAT)

Ea ....... amplitude of first exponential (FLOAT)

Ta ....... time constant of first exponential (FLOAT)

Eb ....... amplitude of second exponential (FLOAT)

Tb ....... time constant of second exponential (FLOAT)

Ec ....... amplitude of third exponential (FLOAT)

Tc ....... time constant of third exponential (FLOAT)

errEa .... error in amplitude of first exponential (FLOAT)

errTa .... error in time constant of first exponential (FLOAT)

errEb .... error in amplitude of second exponential (FLOAT)

errTb .... error in time constant of second exponential (FLOAT)

errEc .... error in amplitude of third exponential (FLOAT)

errTc .... error in time constant of third exponential (FLOAT)

x ........ x axis of supplied data (VEC)

y ........ y axis of supplied data (VEC)

noise .... rms noise of supplied data (FLOAT)

report ... whether to list all results in the CLI. (STR yes/no)
