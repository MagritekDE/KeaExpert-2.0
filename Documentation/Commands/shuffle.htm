shuffle

         

Randomly reorder the contents of a list or a vector

 

Syntax

 

output = shuffle(input)

 

input ...... object to be shuffled (LIST/MAT)

output ..... shuffled object (LIST/MAT)

 

 

Examples

 

   > shuffle([1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9]) =

 

4   7   6   1   2   8   9   3   5

 

   > shuffle(["item1","item2","item3","item4"])

 

item4

item2

item3

item1

  

   > shuffle([1,2,3;4,5,6;7,8,9]) =

 

6   7   4

8   9   5

1   2   3
