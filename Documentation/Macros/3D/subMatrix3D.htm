submatrix3D

 

This macro provides a simple way of extracting part of a 3D data set. For example, in the following case a 20 by 12 by 16 box in the middle of the 3D dataset "m3d" has been extracted and copied to the new matrix m3d_smx.

 

In this case the size and position of the box in the x-y plane was chosen by drawing a rectangle, while the limits in the z direction were entered manually.
