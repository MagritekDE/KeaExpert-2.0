submatrix2D

 

Use this macro to make the visible data, or the data in the selection rectangle the complete data set, throwing away all other data.

 

 

Once the coordinates for your submatrix have been defined in the GUI window, press “Extract” to generate the submatrix. Notice that the numbers in the textboxes are matrix indices – not user defined units.

 

Press “Update” to reload the submatrix size and position. This should normally be done automatically when you load a new image, zoom into a region or draw a rectangle.

 

Press “Undo” to reverse the operation.

 

Note that you can force a selected region to be a power of 2 by holding down the shift key while the region is being selected.
