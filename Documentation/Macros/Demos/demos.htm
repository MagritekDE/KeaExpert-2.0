Demos

 

These macros demonstrate some of the functionality in Prospa

 

Detail for all the options are shown below.

 

helmholtz_coil .......  uses the BiotSavart module to simulate the field around a Helmholtz pair coil.

lines_test ................ generates a rotating 3D graphic. Press escape to abort.

main_demo ............ a series of NMR results are displayed in an infinite loop. Press escape to abort. 

maxwell_coil ......... uses the BiotSavart module to simulate the field around a Maxwell pair coil.

segments_test ........  displays a solenoidal coil as a 3D graphic

vector_test ............. displays a 2D vector field plot. (The E field between two charges).
